Adam Simms is a technology leader, educator, and artist.

He designs Web3 governance protocols and collaboration tools to help communities, teams, and companies become Decentralized Autonomous Organizations.

Teaches As Seen on Screens: Cyberspace & Image Economy in the Photography Program of the Department of Studio Arts at Concordia University.

Creates immersive experiences that combine photography, new media, and the Internet of Things to explore the Newfoundland identity.

/ Worked as a product design contributor, manager, and leader at companies such as Trello, Atlassian, and Lightspeed

/ Holds an MFA in Photography and BFA in Design from Concordia University

/ Is a Post-Image Cluster research member within the Milieux Institute for Arts, Culture and Technology.
